Goals
Quickly browse available products
Order online for convenience
Earn loyalty rewards

Fast search system

As a customer, I want to browse locally farmed food and drink products through an organised online catalogue so that I can easily find items that I want to purchase. The system should allow me to view product descriptions, pricing, and images so that I can make informed purchasing decisions before adding items to my basket.

As a customer, I want to add products to a shopping basket and review them before checkout so that I can purchase multiple items in a single order. This feature should allow me to adjust quantities, remove products, and view the total price before completing the purchase.

As a customer, I want to earn loyalty points whenever I make purchases through the platform so that I am rewarded for regularly buying locally sourced products. The system should keep track of my loyalty balance and allow me to redeem rewards or discounts.

As a customer with visual impairment, I want accessibility features such as screen reader compatibility, adjustable text sizes, and high contrast viewing options so that I can browse and purchase products without difficulty. This ensures the platform is inclusive and usable for people with different accessibility needs.

As a local farmer supplying produce to a company, I want to know that my products are correctly represented on the platform so that customers can clearly see where the products originate from. This helps promote transparency and supports the traceability of locally farmed goods.

As a farmer working with a company, I want my products to be traceable through the inventory system so that the organisation can track where items have come from and how they move through the supply chain. This helps maintain quality control and food safety standards.

As a farmer partnered with a company, I want the platform to clearly display information about local producers so that customers can understand the origin of the products they are purchasing and feel more confident about supporting local agriculture.

As a supplier providing food and drink products to a company, I want the inventory system to accurately record deliveries and stock levels so that the organisation can manage product availability efficiently. This ensures that products remain available to customers without overstocking or shortages.

As a supplier, I want a company staff to be able to update product listings quickly when new stock arrives so that the online shop always reflects the most accurate and up-to-date information for customers browsing the platform.

As a supplier, I want the system to maintain product traceability records so that the origin and movement of each item can be tracked. This is important for maintaining transparency and meeting food safety regulations.

As a a company staff member, I want to add, edit, and remove product listings from the online shop so that the platform always reflects the products currently available for purchase. This allows staff to manage stock and ensure customers see accurate information.

As a a company inventory manager, I want to update stock levels within the system so that customers can see whether products are available before placing an order. This reduces the risk of overselling items that are out of stock.

As a a company staff member responsible for inventory, I want the platform to record traceability data for each product so that the source and distribution of food items can be tracked effectively.

As a a company marketing staff member, I want access to sales insights and analytics so that I can understand which products are most popular with customers and plan future promotions accordingly.
